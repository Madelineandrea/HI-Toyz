Our History

Established in 2019, the store's founders, Sarah and John Thompson, shared a lifelong passion for toys and collectibles. They recognized the need for a dedicated space where enthusiasts could explore and acquire a wide range of toys, mainly figurines.


Sarah and Michael were inspired by their own experiences as collectors, having spent years attending conventions, scouring online marketplaces, and traveling to various stores to find the items they loved. They saw an opportunity to create a haven for collectors in their local community, where they could offer a curated selection of toys and provide a welcoming environment for like-minded individuals to connect and share their passion.
